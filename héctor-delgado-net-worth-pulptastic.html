Héctor Delgado Net Worth: $3 Million

Héctor Delgado, also known as Hector “El Father” or Hector “El Bambino”, is a Puerto Rican rapper, singer, and record producer with a net worth of $3 million. He was born in Carolina, Puerto Rico and was a member of the reggaetón duo Hector & Tito, which released several successful albums including A La Reconquista and Lo de Antes. He released his debut solo album The Bad Boy in 2006, which reached #2 on the US Latin chart, and followed up with Juicio Final in 2008. His single “Sola” reached #1 on the US Latin and US Tropical charts.
