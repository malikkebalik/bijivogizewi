Stephen Roark Gyllenhaal Wiki Biography

Stephen Gyllenhaal was born on October 4, 1949 in Cleveland, Ohio, USA as Stephen Roark Gyllenhaal. He is a director and writer, known for Homegrown (1998), Losing Isaiah (1995) and Paris Trout (1991). He has been married to Kathleen Man since July 23, 2011. He was previously married to Naomi Foner. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
