Joseph Carney Obituary, Terre Haute, IN Longtime Native Has Passed Away

Publish date: 2024-09-01

Joseph Carney Obituary, Death – It is with heavy hearts that we announce the passing of Joseph John Carney of Terre Haute, Indiana, who departed from this world on October 5, 2023, at the age of 89. As we come together to mourn his loss, we also celebrate the rich and meaningful life he led, leaving behind cherished memories for family and friends to hold dear.

Joseph was born to Bernard Carney and Marguerite Hegarty Carney and was predeceased by his brother, James Carney, and sister-in-law, Marian Carney. He is survived by his loving wife, Barbara Kasper Carney, and their daughters, Kathleen Carney Marshall of Cincinnati and Janet Carney Cooper (Timothy) of Terre Haute, Indiana. Joseph’s legacy lives on in the hearts of his grandchildren, Emily Marshall Greenspan (Brett) of Chicago, John Marshall of Chicago, Maxwell Cooper (Katie) of Kansas City, and Mallory Cooper Cox (Ethan) of Terre Haute, as well as his great-grandchild, Grace Greenspan. He is also survived by his brother, Bernard W. Carney, and his cousin, Jane Ann Faubion of Terre Haute, along with several nieces and nephews.

Joseph’s life was a testament to love, family, and community. His warm presence and generous spirit touched the lives of many. As we remember him, let us also celebrate the moments of joy and the lasting impact he had on those fortunate enough to know him.

Visitation will be held on Monday, October 9th, 2023, from 12:00 PM to 1:00 PM at St. Joseph University Parish (113 S 5th St, Terre Haute, IN 47807), followed by a Mass of Christian Burial at the same location at 1:00 PM.

In lieu of flowers, the family suggests donations to Saint Joseph University Parish Food Pantry or Gentiva Hospice, honoring Joseph’s commitment to giving back to the community that meant so much to him. May Joseph John Carney rest in eternal peace, and may his memory be a source of comfort and inspiration to all who knew and loved him.
