Nicole Kang is an American actress who is best known for being cast as Lynn Lieser in You (2018) and Mary Hamilton in Batwoman. She is also known for her appearances in several other shows such as The Code (2019) and Orange Is the New Black (2019). Nicole is also noted for portraying important characters in theatrical plays such as The Laramie Project, Proof, A Doll’s House, and A Midsummer Night’s Dream. She has amassed a huge fan base with more than 100k followers on Instagram.

Education

Nicole graduated with a degree in Bachelor of Arts from Case Western Reserve University where she studied under the careful guidance of Heather Anderson Boll.

She also attended several summer sessions at The Performing Arts Project where she was taught by Susan Meisner, Jonathan Bernstein, Monica Raymund, and Susan Blackwell.

Then, she moved on to attain a Bachelor of Fine Arts from New York University’s Tisch School of the Arts and trained at the Stella Adler Studio of Acting.

In 2018, she was attending dance lessons at the Broadway Dance Center in New York.
