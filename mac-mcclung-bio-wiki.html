Mac McClung Bio (Wiki)

Publish date: 2024-08-09

Who is Mac McClung?

Mac McClung is a college basketball player. He is renowned for his playing position as a point guard.

Currently, he plays for the Texas Tech University’s basketball team, Texas Tech Red Raiders. 

Mac McClung- Birth, Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Siblings

Mac McClung was born as Matthew McClung on the 6th of January 2000 in Gate City, Virginia.

His father, Marcus McClung is an attorney who serves as Commonwealth Attorney for Scott County, Virginia.

Whereas, his mother, Lenoir McClung is a teacher at Gate City High.

He has an elder sister, Anna McClung. Anna is in high school and pursuing soccer as a profession. Currently, she happens to be VHSL’s top scorer of all-time.

Education

In 2017, he graduated from the Gate City High School in his hometown, Gate City. After that, in 2018, he joined Georgetown University.

However, he left the university in 2020 and got enrolled at Texas Tech University.

Mac McClung- Basketball Career

The basketball player grew up as a competitive child. First, he started playing football.

However, he developed a keen interest when he was in grade seven. Since then, he is continuing playing basketball.

However, his initial journey as a basketball player started during his High School days at Gate City High School. Back in those days, he was famous for his acrobatic dunker.

At the time, he played in Virginia High School League where he scored career-high 64 points.

Also, he was the winner of the slam dunk contest in the Ballislife All-American Game.

Moving forward, on the 22nd of December 2018, Mac McClung got enrolled at Georgetown University. Also, he played for the basketball team at the university.

During his freshman year, he scored a season-high score of 38 points. Later, his name also included Big East All-Freshman Team.

In the sophomore season, he got a foot injury causing him to miss several matches. After that, he announced that he will be signing for the 2020 NBA draft.

On the 27th of March 2020, he switched to Texas Tech University. Also, he would represent the basketball team of the university.
