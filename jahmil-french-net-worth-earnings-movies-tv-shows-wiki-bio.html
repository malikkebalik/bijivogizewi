Jahmil French is a rising Canadian television and film actor who made many fans fall in love with him after his excellent works in the entertainment industry.

Jahmil French’s Net Worth

Jahmil French holds a net worth of $300,000. He garnered that fortune from his career as a television and film actor.

Little did you know, the Canadian started his career nearly a decade ago. The actor first got a chance to perform in the 2009 television series, Flashpoint. After appearing in the series, he grabbed the attention of many viewers and producers. Thanks to that, he then acted in many trending television series like Degrassi: Minis, The Divide, Remedy, and Let’s Get Physical.

The actor, moreover, is about to star in the upcoming 2019 television series, Soundtrack. Further, he will be seen portraying the role of Dante Mendoza in the Netflix series. The series is set to premiere on Netflix on December 18, 2019. Furthermore, the series will also star actors Callie Hernandez and Jenna Dewan. Until now, the actor worked along with actors like Aislinn Paul, Cassie Steele, and Stacey Farber.

The 28 years old actor also performed in various films in his career. Starting from the 2012 short film, Jamestown as Duane, he worked in more than nine films as of 2019. Some of his famous television and feature films are Rocky Road, Boost, At First Light, The Night Before Halloween. Further, he will also star in the upcoming short film, Dalia as Rock in 2019.

Dave Turner in Degrassi: The Next Generation

Among all the performances of Jahmil, he is best known for his work in the television series, Degrassi: The Next Generation. The series is about a Canadian teen drama television series which is the follow up of the Degrassi franchise. Until now, the show consists of 387 episodes under its 14 seasons cycle.

French joined in the ninth season of the series and portrayed the role of Dave Turner. The actor worked as one of the lead actors in the series for four seasons totaling 145 episodes.
