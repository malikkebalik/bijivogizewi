Summer was reported missing from her Hawkins County home on the evening of June 15, 2021. The following morning, an Amber Alert was issued for the girl. Leslie Earhart, a spokesperson for the Tennessee Bureau of Investigation, told reporters during a press conference that the circumstances surrounding Summer’s disappearance remain unclear, as it would have been uncharacteristic for Summer to wander away from home on her own.

Despite several leads and tips provided early in the investigation, nearly two years later, Summer remains missing and the AMBER Alert continues to be in effect for her whereabouts.

Is Summer Wells Found In 2023?

Summer Wells is not found till date and her investigation is still ongoing. She has been missing for over a year now and despite the efforts of the police and her family, no trace of her has been found. Her case has been opened as a criminal investigation as there is evidence to suggest that foul play was involved in her disappearance.

Summer Wells Age

Summer Wells was 7 years old at the time of her missing. She was last seen on June 15 in the Beach Creek Community of Hawkins County, outside of her family’s home.

According to her father, she went home alone around 6:30 p.m. because she wanted to go downstairs and play with her toys. When he went down to the basement she was gone.

Summer Wells Father | Donald Wells

Summer Wells’ father, who was in jail for DUI charges since June, 2021, is breaking his silence after being released from jail in connection to the disappearance of his daughter.

According to Donald Wells, the Wells family is still committed to the search for Summer, the 7-year-old girl who went missing after picking flowers with her mother and grandmother in June 2021. In addition, he pointed out that the family will not give up on finding her no matter what.
