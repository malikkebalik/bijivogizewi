Cardi B Shows Off Sickening Pink Custom-Made Piano

Publish date: 2024-07-20

Cardi B is known for living a lavish lifestyle, and she’s now leveled up her home decor with a “sickening” custom pink piano.

On Friday (June 23), the “Bodak Yellow” rhymer took to Twitter where she gave fans a glimpse of her expanding instrument collection, including an impressive pink, acrylic, and rose gold grand piano.

“My piano is crazy,” the “I Like It,” rapper says in the clip. “Look at my piano guys,” she adds, as she slowly zooms in on her prized possession, complete with a matching pink tufted bench with acrylic trim and rose gold legs.

“My piano is fckin SICKENING. Custom made for ME,” she captioned the post, topping it off with a piano and sparkling heart emoji.

It’s been a week of celebrations for Cardi who earlier this week witnessed her firstborn, Kulture, graduate from pre-kindergarten.

On Thursday (June 22), the “WAP” MC posted photographs of her daughter on Instagram to commemorate the completion of her 4-year-old’s first stage of schooling. In the images, the little one can be seen wearing a blue cap and gown and holding a certificate that reads: “Award Certificate Celebrating Kulture Cephus for the Best Dance Moves.”
