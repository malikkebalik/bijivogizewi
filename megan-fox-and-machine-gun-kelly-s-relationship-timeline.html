Megan Fox and Machine Gun Kellys Relationship Timeline

Publish date: 2024-07-12

Megan Fox and Machine Gun Kelly's relationship has people wondering if they are expecting a child after MGK's cryptic message at the Billboard Music Awards.

Machine Gun Kelly surprised the world while performing "Twin Flame" at the Billboard Music Awards in Las Vegas on May 15, 2022. Apart from saying he wrote the song for his "wife," the rapper-turned-punk rocker mentioned their "unborn child."

Rumors spread quickly on Twitter. Netizens claimed that the heartbeat-like sound in "Twin Flame" was another subtle reference to Megan Fox's alleged pregnancy. But are Machine Gun Kelly and Megan Fox married?

Megan Fox and Machine Gun Kelly on September 12, 2021 in New York City | Source: Getty Images

HOW LONG HAVE MEGAN FOX AND MACHINE GUN KELLY BEEN TOGETHER?

MGK and Fox's love story started like most in Hollywood: on a movie set. They met in March 2020 in Puerto Rico while shooting "Midnight in the Switchgrass." Fox was still married to "Beverly Hills, 90210" actor Brian Austin Green.

Green confirmed she and Fox parted ways in May 2020. Around that time, the "Transformers" actress and the "Concert for Aliens" singer were spotted in Los Angeles.

Megan Fox and Machine Gun Kelly on April 24, 2021 in Jacksonville, Florida | Source: Getty Images

Machine Gun Kelly's "Bloody Valentine" music video, released in May 2020, had Megan Fox as his love interest. They had so many steamy scenes together that everyone was convinced they were dating.

The couple confirmed their relationship the following month when photographers caught them kissing. Fox later referred to MGK as her "twin flame" because she felt like they were two halves of the same soul as soon as they met.

Fox and MGK sparked engagement rumors in January 2021 when she wore a ring on her left hand. However, they got engaged on January 11, 2022.

DO MEGAN FOX AND MACHINE GUN KELLY HAVE CHILDREN TOGETHER?

Machine Gun Kelly and Megan Fox don't have kids together, but they have children from previous relationships. The rapper's daughter, Casie Colson Baker, was born in July 2009 during his relationship with Emma Cannon.

On the other hand, Fox has three kids with her ex-husband, Brian Austin Green: Noah (September 2012), Bodhi (February 2014), and Journey (August 2016).

Casie and Fox allegedly have a beautiful relationship, and the couple would love to have their four kids involved in their wedding because they know they will blend their families.

Fox supposedly wants Casie to be part of her bridal party, but she has not shared the roles her sons will play. Sources claim that Green is "very supportive" of his sons' involvement in the wedding.

WHO IS THE FATHER OF MEGAN FOX'S CHILDREN?

Brian Austin Green is the father of Megan Fox's kids. He revealed on his podcast, "…with Brian Austin Green," that he and Fox parted ways in December 2019 after she realized she felt "more like [her]self" without him.

In January 2021, Green and Australian ballroom dancer Sharna Burgess made their relationship Instagram official. In February 2022, Fox and Green reached a divorce settlement.

Only a week later, Green and Burgess announced they were expecting their first kid together. Their boy is expected to be born in July 2022. Their blended family keeps growing!
