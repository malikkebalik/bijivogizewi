Quincy Isaiah Biography

On May 29, 1994, Quincy Isaiah was born in Tulsa, Oklahoma, in the United States. He learned to play the saxophone at a young age because he was raised in a musical family. After graduating from high school, Quincy decided to pursue acting as a career. He studied acting at the University of Central Oklahoma, where he received a Bachelor of Fine Arts.

Quincy Isaiah Height And Weight

The height of Quincy Isaiah is 6 feet 2 inches (1.88 meters), and his weight is roughly 183 pounds (83 kg).

Quincy Isaiah Career

In 2016, Quincy Isaiah began performing, making appearances in stage shows and short films. His performance in the play “The Gospel of Lovingkindness,” which was presented at the National Black Theatre Festival, earned him praise. He debuted on television in the 2018 season of “Merry Happy Whatever.”

Quincy was cast as Zeke in the Netflix series “Chadwick Boseman: Portrait of an Artist” in 2020, which became his breakout role. The program honored the late actor and highlighted his life and accomplishments. Quincy became recognized as a rising star in Hollywood thanks to the positive reviews he earned for his portrayal of Boseman.

Quincy has acted in commercials for companies like Calvin Klein and Ralph Lauren in addition to his work in television and movies.
