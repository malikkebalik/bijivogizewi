Marcus Rosemy is an NCAA footballer playing #1 wide receiver for Georgia Bulldogs. After a string of stellar performances in high school and college, he has risen to prominence.

Marcus will presumably play when his team, the Georgia Bulldogs, takes on the South Carolina Gamecocks at Williams Brice Stadium on September 17.

What is Marcus Rosemy’s age? Wikipedia And Footballer’s Age

Marcus Rosemy’s date of birth and birth year are unknown, but we can estimate that he is between 22 and 25 years old.

Marcus was born in Fort Lauderdale’s Pompano Beach. Rosemy is an American whose complete name is Marcus David Rosemy-Jacksaint.

Marcus attended high school at St. Thomas Aquinas. There, he participated in high school football under the direction of Roger Harriott. He intends to pursue a business degree in college.

The student from the class of 2020 has been awarded the Aubrey Cecil Rhodes Sr. Football Scholarship.

Antonio Brown, an NFL wide receiver, praised Marcus’s 70-yard touchdown catch with one hand against De La Salle HS, for which he was praised by NFL wide receiver Marcus.

How did Marcus arrive at his current position?

Marcus has received scholarship offers from the Penn State Nittany Lions, Kentucky Wildcats, Arkansas Razorbacks, Auburn Tigers, Notre Dame Fighting Irish, and Texas A&M Aggies since 2018. He made official and unofficial visits to a few of them.

He ultimately committed to the Georgia Bulldogs on June 20, 2019, and matriculated there on June 5, 2020, a year later.

According to 247 Sports, Marcus is a 4-star prospect with a 0.9734 composite rating. The website positions him 55th nationally, eighth among wide receivers and tenth among Florida recruits.

The juvenile footballer has played for Great Britain for three years (2020-present).

Marcus has recorded three receptions, 30 receiving yards, 10 yards per reception, and 12 long receptions during the 2022 season. His coach at present is Kirby Smart.

In addition, Marcus adores seafood, and his favorite dish is crab legs; he claims he could consume them every day if he could.

Marcus Rosemy has a height of 6 feet 2 inches and a weight of 195 pounds.
