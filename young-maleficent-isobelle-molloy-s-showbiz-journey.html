Young Maleficent Isobelle Molloy's Showbiz Journey

Publish date: 2024-07-13

Have you watched the Disney Movie, Maleficent? If you have, you would definitely recognize Isobelle Molloy. She played the role of the young Maleficent.

But that is not all about the young actress. Know more about Isobelle below.

Net Worth of a Young Actress

The Maleficent star has an estimated net worth of $100 thousand. She accumulated the amount from appearing in TV shows such as EastEnders as Bella Young and Children in Need. Additionally, she also acted as Holly in the movie, Una.

Isobelle Molloy is also quite known for her role as Matilda Wormwood at the Cambridge Theatre. Interestingly, she has played alongside actors like Angelina Jolie, Tamzin Outhwaite, Danniella Westbrook, and such.

Maleficent

The dark fantasy film, Maleficent was released in 2014. She starred as the younger self of the world’s highest-paid actress Angelina Jolie. With Joile as the lead actress in the movie, it smashed a total box-office collection of $758 million.

Isobelle appears in the movie when Maleficent was younger and creates a rather close bond with a boy. The boy later betrays her and cuts off her wings leading him to be the next ruler of the kingdom. Raged by the betrayal she shuts herself in a mysterious jungle away from all human reach. She then grows into the role played by Angelina.

Molloy’s Early Days

Molloy spent her early days with her mother, Leanne Cornwell alongside a brother. She studied in Tomorrow’s Talent theatre school located in Danbury. She completed her Bishops’ C of E and R C Primary School.

The 13-year-old was given a scholarship at the Redroofs Theatre School considering her role as Matilda. She then moved to Maidenhead to attend Redroofs.

Social Media Incident

The use of social media has grown drastically with time. It has advantages but disadvantages likewise. Around the year 2013, she grabbed major attention when an imposter created her fake account when she was only 13 years old.

Questions and confusion arose when her fellow stars received unusual messages through her fake account. Her family was notified of the incident and was in equal turmoil.

The 13-year-old then decided to create her own twitter account and hence got verified as an official account. She further added that social media is great medium to exchange information but it can also be toxic and lead to mishaps.

Quick Facts on Molloy

  • She started her career as a dancer when she was only three.
  • Molloy began acting from the age of 8.
  • She acted as Amanda Thripp, a pig-tailed schoolgirl in the West End version of Matilda the Musical when she was only 11 years of age.
  • Her nick-name is Issy.
