Caleb Farley Siblings: Elder Brother Joshua Was Also An Athlete

Publish date: 2024-07-21

The American football cornerback Caleb Farley does not have multiple siblings but rather only one elder brother, Joshua.

He serves as the younger counterpart to Joshua Farley, who possesses a background in athletics.

Following the unfortunate loss of their mother in 2018, they have more recently had to bear the sorrow of their father’s passing.

Caleb Ray Farley is a 25-year-old football player hailing from Maiden, North Carolina.

The youngster attended his native Maiden High School and played as a quarterback. His excellent potential led him to Virginia Tech College.

Unfortunately, he missed his freshman season because of an ACL injury. However, that didn’t stop him from being named to the 2019 first-team All-ACC.

The Titans selected him as the 22nd overall pick in the first round of the 2021 NFL Draft.

He suffered a season-ending torn ACL injury again in week 6. The wave of injuries didn’t stop, as he suffered from a herniated disk in the 2022 season.

Does Caleb Farley Have Any Siblings? Meet Joshua Farley

The cornerback, Caleb, grew up with an elder brother named Joshua Farley. Caleb’s elder brother, Joshua, was born two years after their parent’s marriage. 

On the other hand, Caleb was born ten years after Joshua’s arrival.

Joshua attended Appalachian State University, where he competed in Division 1 track and field.

However, he later transitioned out of the athletic life and built his career in project management.

Joshua has over 10 years of experience in various technical and business environments.

In addition to that, he is a skilled professional who has excelled in management and business analysis.

Currently, Joshua has been working as a senior project manager of technology at HIMSS since March 2022.

Furthermore, despite not displaying it to the public, the relationship between the two brothers is formidable.

Joshua was also present during his younger brother’s high school graduation ceremony.

Who Are Caleb Farley Parents? A Tragic Story

As mentioned earlier, the young NFL player is the second son of Robert and Robin Farley. He grew up in a religious and athletic household with his parents and brother.

Unfortunately, both his parents are no longer with us in this world. Caleb has had to deal with the death of his mother and father just five years apart.

Caleb’s mother, Robin, died in 2018 due to breast cancer, seriously affecting young Caleb and making him numb.

He acknowledges his mother for helping him grow stronger by instilling faith and belief in him.

After his mother’s death, Caleb and his father got closer and built a stronger bond. They started living together near Caleb’s college.

“I just wanted him to know there was someone in his life who had always been there so he could be comfortable and not be overwhelmed by the situation,” Robert Farley said.

But sadly, on August 22, 2023, Caleb’s father, Robert, became a victim of a gas leak explosion and died in his home in Mooresville, North Carolina.

Moreover, fans and the whole of the NFL franchise are pouring their deepest condolences to Caleb and his close ones.
