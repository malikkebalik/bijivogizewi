Jenny Anchondo

Publish date: 2024-09-15

Jenny Anchondo is a prominent American Emmy Award-winning anchor who is currently working for Fox 4 news network along with Melaine Lawson. Similarly, she is also equally famous as a public orator, literacy campaigner, and a humanitarian.

Heartbreaking news for Anchondo fans and followers, this beautiful anchor is a married woman as of 2019. Likewise, she also shares a child with her husband. So, people are more curious to know about her husband and child. Let's have a glance over her marital life.

Husband - Heath Oakes

The Emmy Award-winning journalist, Jenny Anchondo is a happily married woman. Unlike other celebrities who keep their personal life secret, she doesn't hesitate to flaunt her spouse to the public. Jenny is in a marital relationship with Heath Oakes, who is an author.

The two of them first met at the gym, where they had their first introduction. Right after their first meeting, the two started dating, and Heath even began visiting Jenny's parents often. As per some sources, her husband proposed her on a rooftop, and the couple got engaged afterward.

Further, Jenny married her beau, Heath Oakes on 18 June 2016. Since then, the married couple is living a happy married life without any kind of divorce and separation rumors.

Blessed With A Child

From their blissful marital relationship, the married couple shares a child, a baby daughter, Brighton, born in May 2019. Jenny often uploads pictures of her daughter on her Instagram account. As can be seen, a cute daughter is now grown up enough to stand at her own feet.

Besides, her family includes a cute dog of breed, terrier. At present, they are happily residing together and haven't planned to add a new member to their family.

Career - Fox 4 & Podcast

Jenny kicked off her career by working for KNDO TV as an anchor. Later, she left the job in 2005 and joined KNDU TV and worked from 2005 to 2007. Similarly, she also worked at WXIN TV from 2007 to 2010.

Again, she got promoted to a Morning Reporter for WXIN TV from 2010 to 2013. Later on 2013, she started working as an anchor at Fox 4 news network alongside Diana Zoga and Natalie Solis.

As a Body Builder and Fitness Instructor

Before pursuing a career in the field of news anchoring, she was a fitness instructor and personal trainer for more than a decade.

Being a fitness freak, she has a well-maintained body figure despite being in her early 40s. Still, she often hit the gym to keep her body figure well-maintained and follow a strict diet.
