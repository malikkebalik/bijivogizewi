Amy Davidson Biography- Married, Husband, Son, Net Worth, Age, Height, Blogger

Publish date: 2024-08-25

Amy Davidson is an American actress who became a household name in the early 2000s through the TV series '8 Simple Rules' and 'So Little Time.'

Over time, Davidson has maintained her popularity through steady work as an actress and as an influential blogger. Learn more about this talented redhead below, including her married life and children.

Amy Davidson is Married! Learn about her Husband

Amy Davidson is happily married to Kacy Lockwood. He is a professional who works as an Operations Manager at Systems Application & Technologies, Inc.(SA-TECH INC). He also has years of experience as a boat captain.

Davidson regularly shares pictures of her husband on her Instagram. However, Mr. Lockwood is more of a private person and is not available on any social media platforms.

Amy Davidson and Her Husband Had Destination Wedding

Amy Davidson tied the Knot with Kacy Lockwood on May 1, 2020, in Mexico. The wedding took place at Grand Coral Beach Club, Playa del Carmen. 

The couple kept the ocean as the theme of the wedding, and hence bridesmaid and bridegroom wore colors that complemented the theme. The bridesmaids wore coral blue-colored dresses, and the bridegrooms wore a white suit with a coral blue-colored tie.

As for the bride, she wore a voluminous ivory dress with a sweetheart neckline. Whereas the groom dressed in an all-white suit with a matching tie.

Amy Davidson Shares A Son with her Husband

Amy Davidson and her spouse welcomed their first and only child, Lennox Sawyer Lockwood, on March 1, 2016. He was born 18 days before his due date but did not suffer any major health complications.

Unlike some celebrity parents, Amy has not kept her son away from the media spotlight. She regularly shares pictures of him on her Instagram and also mentions him on her blogs.

Amy Davidson's Net Worth

Amy Davidson is active in showbiz for over two decades. She debuted in 2000 with the TV Movie 'The Truth about Jane.'  

Since then, the Arizona native has worked on 40 projects, including 'So Little Time' and '8 Simple Rules. Most recently, she was seen in the TV series 'All Rise' as 'Kelsey Strong.'

Amy Davidson's Wiki-Bio

Amy Davidson was born on September 15, 1979, and as of the writing of this article, is 41-years-of age. Her birthplace is Phoenix, Arizona, and she is a US Citizen.

While growing up, Davidson trained in various dance forms such as ballet, hip hop, and modern dancing. Also, she participated in various national-level dance competitions.
