Kirby Howell-Baptiste Facts

  • In 2013, she worked at the director, writer, and producer for the short horror film, Lucky Day.
  • Kirby Howell-Baptiste played the role of Maya, Tino’s owner, in the 2017 American comedy-drama adventure film, A Dog’s Purpose, which starred Britt Robertson, KJ Apa, John Ortiz, Dennis Quaid, and Josh Gad.
  • She was cast as one of the lead cast members, alongside Allison Tolman, Ned, Lucas Neff, and Barry Rothbart, in the comedy TV series, Downward Dog, in 2017.
  • She co-starred as Elena Felton, Eve’s assistant, in the spy thriller dark comedy television series, Killing Eve, in 2018.
  • Kirby Howell-Baptiste is also known for portraying the character of Simone Garnett, an Australian neuroscientist who becomes Chidi’s girlfriend for a brief duration, in the philosophical fantasy-comedy dystopian television series, The Good Place, whose lead cast comprised of actors Kristen Bell, William Jackson Harper, Jameela Jamil, D’Arcy Carden, Manny Jacinto, and Ted Danson.
  • In 2019, she appeared in the leading role of Taylor Harding, a bis*xual, feminist attorney in an open marriage with Eli, in the American dark comedy-drama web television series, Why Women Kill.
  • Kirby Howell-Baptiste has done voiceover work in shows such as The Powerpuff Girls, TripTank, Big City Greens, and Infinity Train.
