Justin Bieber Bio (Wiki)

Publish date: 2024-08-18

Who is Justin Bieber?

Justin Bieber is a singer as well as a songwriter. His full name is Justin Drew Bieber and he belongs to the Canadian community. In 2010, he released his first full-length studio album, My World 2.0.

Furthermore, he had won numerous awards throughout his career, including the American Music Award for Artist of the Year in 2010 and 2012, a Grammy Award for Best Dance in the 2016 ceremony, and a Latin Grammy Award for Best Urban Fusion/Performance for the remix of Despacito at the 2017 ceremony.

 Justin Bieber: Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, Education

Justin Bieber was born on March 1st, 1994 in Ontario, Canada. He is the son of father Jeremy Jack Bieber and mother Patricia “Pattie” Mallette.

He has two siblings named Jaxon Bieber (Half Brother), and Jazmyn Bieber (Younger Half-Sister).  His parents had never married and he was raised by his mother, Diane, and stepfather, Bruce.

He had attended an elementary school in the Jeanne Sauvé Catholic School. In 2012, he graduated from high school in Stratford, Ontario, the St. Michael Catholic Secondary School.

When he was 12 years old, he sang Ne-Yo’s “So Sick” for a local singing competition in Stratford and was placed second.

Health

Justin tested positive for COVID-19 on February 20, 2022, two days after embarking on his fourth concert tour, the Justin Word Tour.

In June of the same year, Bieber revealed on Instagram and Tiktok that he had Ramsay Hunt Syndrome and half of his face was paralyzed due to which he canceled concerts and appearance and stated that he doesn’t know how long it will take for him to recover.

Justin Bieber: Career, Awards, and Net Worth

Moving towards his career life, Justin Bieber started in 2008. This year, he signed to Raymond Braun Media Group (RBMG), a joint venture between Braun and Usher.

The next year, he released his first single, “One Time” which reached number 12 on the Canadian Hot 100 during its first week.

In the same year, he performed on several live shows such as MTV’s VMA 09 Tour, European program The Dome, YTV’s The Next Star, The Today Show, and BET’s 106 & Park.

Subsequently, he released his debut album, My World 2.0 which featured Ludacris, and became an international hit.

Then he appeared on several live programs including The View, the 2010 Kids’ Choice Awards, Nightline, Late Show, and 106 & Park to promote his album in the same year.

The next year, he had begun his third studio album, entitled Believe, released in 2012. The same year, he appeared on The Ellen DeGeneres Show to announce that the first single would be called “Boyfriend“.

Moreover, he had announced that he would release a new song every Monday for 10 weeks as a lead-up to the film Justin Bieber’s Believe in 2013.

In the same year, he fainted backstage at London’s O2 Arena after complaining of breathing problems throughout his performance.

Likewise, he released a song “Where Are Ü Now”, with Jack Ü and  “What Do You Mean? in 2015. Then he released a new single with Major Lazer and MØ titled “Cold Water” in 2016.

The next year, he released the single “Friends” with American record producer and songwriter BloodPop.

On April 29, 2022, Bieber released a new single called Honest which featured Don Toliver as well as a music video directed by Cole Bennett.

Through his hard work in his professional life, he has earned a net worth of $225 million.

He has even won numerous awards throughout his career, including the American Music Award for Artist of the Year in 2010 and 2012, a Grammy Award for Best Dance in the 2016 ceremony, and a Latin Grammy Award for Best Urban Fusion/Performance for the remix of Despacito at the 2017 ceremony.
