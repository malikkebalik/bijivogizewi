Is Kesha Still Alive? The Truth Behind the Rumors

In this article, we will explore the facts and the fiction behind the question: is Kesha still alive?

Kesha’s Legal Struggle with Dr. Luke

One of the most prominent and publicized events in Kesha’s life was her lawsuit against her former producer and label owner, Dr. Luke. Kesha accused him of sexually, physically, and emotionally abusing her for over a decade, and sought to be released from her contract with him. Dr. Luke denied the allegations and countersued Kesha for defamation and breach of contract.

The legal dispute lasted for several years, and had a significant impact on Kesha’s career and mental health. She was unable to release new music under her own name, and faced a lot of backlash and criticism from some fans and industry insiders. She also suffered from depression, anxiety, and post-traumatic stress disorder, and checked into a rehab facility in 2014 to treat her eating disorder.

The case was eventually settled out of court in June 2023, after a New York judge dismissed most of Kesha’s claims and ruled that she had defamed Dr. Luke. Kesha agreed to pay him $1.3 million in damages, and to drop all remaining charges against him. She also regained full control over her music and career, and was free to work with other producers and labels.

Kesha’s Health Diagnosis and Recovery

Another major challenge that Kesha faced was her diagnosis with common variable immunodeficiency (CVID), a rare disorder that affects the immune system and makes it difficult to fight off infections.
