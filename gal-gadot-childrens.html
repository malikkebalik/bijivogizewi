Gal Gadot rose to stardom as the title character in the 2017 DC comic film Wonder Woman. But, aside from being a stunning actress, Gadot is also a good mother. The former Israeli pageant winner, 34, is the joyful mother of two girls.

Since 2008, Gadot has been married to Israeli real estate entrepreneur and hotelier Yaron Varsano. Even though Gadot has not disclosed much about her marriage, she has been open about her motherhood. So, in today’s episode, we’ll learn about Gal Gadot’s road to becoming a mother of two.

Gal Gadot and Yaron Varsano married secretly in Tel Aviv, Israel, in 2008. During a yoga retreat in 2006, the two met through mutual acquaintances. They got engaged a few months before their wedding in 2008, after dating for a few years.

The couple became parents for the first time three years after their marriage. On November 6, 2011, Gadot gave birth to their first child, a girl named Alma Varsano, in the United States. Gadot was not yet a Hollywood star when she became a mother for the first time, which might explain why the media doesn’t know much about Alma.

Maya and Alma Gadot are Gal Gadot’s two kids.

Furthermore, Gadot does not post images of her children on social media. However, on Alma’s eighth birthday, November 6, 2019, Gadot took to Instagram and wrote a lovely letter about motherhood.

“Eight years ago today, my life changed forever. Alma had had enough and chose to arrive a few weeks before my due date,” Gadot tweeted beside a throwback photo of her and her husband holding their child. Gadot went on,

“She has infused our home with so much love and light.” So much laughing, with cheeky comedic moments, and so much inquiry, both courageous and innocent. I consider myself extremely fortunate to be your mommy.

Thank you for teaching me so much about life without even realizing it and for bestowing upon me the most valuable title I could ever desire.”

“I promise I’ll do anything for you, love and protect you for the rest of my life.” Please, don’t grow up too quickly. Allow yourself plenty of time. I can’t believe you’re already eight years old. “I love you to the moon and through all galaxies twice the size of the universe.”

Gadot became a more responsible woman after becoming a mother.

Gal Gadot, the actress, has repeatedly stated that having children has helped her become a more responsible woman. She has managed to keep her job schedules consistent so that she may devote more time to her children. She once stated, ”

“Because of my celebrity, I have to be extremely organized.” Being a mother, on the other hand, entails waking up early in the morning, even if you’re exhausted, since it is your obligation.

Gadot also expressed a desire to spend more time with her daughter, Alma. So, her mother and daughter used to accompany her on set, so her mother would take after Alma while she worked. The Justice League actress stated that seeing her daughter on work with her mother brought her peace.

Maya Varsano, Gal Gadot’s second daughter

Gal Gadot and her husband Yaron Varsano welcomed their second child, daughter Maya Varsano, on March 17, 2017 in Los Angeles, California. G,adot revealed her pregnancy in November 2016, shortly after wrapping up filming on Wonder Woman.

While filming Wonder Woman, Gadot kept her pregnancy a secret from her fans and most of her Wonder Woman co-stars. Gadot stated in an interview that she concealed her pregnancy because she did not want to be treated differently on work.

Alma, their eldest daughter, knows and speaks both languages. Gal stated:

“Alma is a bilingual woman who understands and speaks both Hebrew and English.” She’s an extrovert. You can only benefit from all of these experiences, meeting new people, and traveling to various places and cultures.”
