Laticia Rolle- Biography

Laticia Rolle is an American model, fashion designer, and entrepreneur best known for her fashion, diet, make-up, and lifestyle blog BluHazl. Furthermore, since she began dating basketball legend Shaquille O’Neal, her name has spread like wildfire.

Laticia Rolle- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings, Education

Laticia Lee Rolle was born in Gardner, Massachusetts, and spent her youth there with her four siblings: two brothers and two sisters. Gisemi and D’Ana are her sisters, and Xavier and Elijah are her brothers. Nancy and Alvarez Rolle were the parents of the five siblings.

Her father, Alvarez, was injured when Laticia was ten years old and was sacked as a result of his injury. The Rolles was going through a rough patch at the time. However, thanks to God, the family received support from GCAC. So far, this fashion icon has completed her high school education at Worcester Academy in Massachusetts, where she graduated in 2006. Apart from that, Rolle attended Eckerd University in 2010 and majored in Business Communication and Management.

Before graduating from Worcester Academy, Laticia played for the women’s basketball team and was voted MVP Player of her team. Meanwhile, this media figure is of mixed race and holds an American passport.
