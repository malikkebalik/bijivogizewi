Léonie Cassel is the daughter of Monica Bellucci an Italian actress and model. Her father Vincent Cassel is a French actor who is known for movies such as Mon Roi (2015), It’s Only the End of the World (2016), and The Specials (2019). Both of her parents are famous. Monica met Vincent Cassel on the set of their film The Apartment (1996) and they tied on 2 August 1999 in Monaco.

They appeared in nine films together between 1996 and 2006. The members of the family dispersed to several countries, including Italy, France, Brazil, and England. On August 26, 2013, the couple announced their divorce and separation. Bellucci confirmed her enduring affection for Cassel at year’s end 2014, and the two have stayed close throughout the years.

How old is Léonie Cassel?

Léonie Cassel was born on 21 May 2010. She is 13 years old, as of 2023. She has a sister named, Deva Cassel (born 12 September 2004). She lives with her mother in England, France and Italy. Later family shifted to France, in 2015. Her mother Bellucci said,

“I am entirely Italian. Everything about me is Italian” and acknowledged that “Paris is part of my history”.

Léonie Cassel Religion

Léonie Cassel is Catholic by religion. Bellucci has made it clear that she is no longer religious, “I come from a Catholic religion, but I’m not Catholic”.

She has called herself an agnostic, saying, “I am an agnostic, even though I respect and am interested in all religions … If there’s something I believe in, it’s a mysterious energy; the one that fills the oceans during tides, the one that unites nature and beings.”

Bellucci claims that her upbringing has had a significant impact on her persona, “Certainly a lot of positivity also depends on the climate in which my parents raised me.”
