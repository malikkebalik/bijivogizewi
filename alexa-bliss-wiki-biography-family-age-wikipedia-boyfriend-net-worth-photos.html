Alexa Bliss Wiki-Lifestyle-Career

Early Career: - Alexis Kaufman was born on August 9, 1991, in Columbus, Ohio, to adolescent parents who were still in high school at the time. She has participated in athletics since she was five years old, participating in track, kickboxing, softball, and gymnastics. She was also a cheerleader at Hilliard Davidson High School and attended the University of Akron, where she earned an M.A. in medical dietetics. She has competed in the Arnold Classic and spent time competing in competitive fitness events. She had a life-threatening eating issue when she was 15, but resorting to fitness contests helped her overcome it.

Career: - Kaufman was signed by WWE in May 2013 despite having no prior wrestling experience and reported to WWE's developmental division NXT. She made her first broadcast appearance on the July 24 edition of NXT (which was recorded on June 20), celebrating the inaugural NXT Women's Champion Paige. She was promoted to WWE.com's NXT roster page in August and given the ring name Alexa Bliss. She appeared as an unidentified ring announcer on NXT's November 20 show. Her first broadcast debut on the main roster occurred on April 6, 2014, at WrestleMania XXX, when she assisted in Triple H's entrance.
