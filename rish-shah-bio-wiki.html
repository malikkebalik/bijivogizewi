Who is Rish Shah?

Rish Shah is a British actor, model, television personality, social media personality, social media influencer, and entrepreneur. He starred as Kamran in “Ms. Marvel” which made him widely famous.

Rish’s breakthrough role as Russ in “Do Revenge” brought him a lot of fame. In the year 2021, he was named Brit to Watch by Variety.

Rish Shah- Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, Education

Rish Shah also known as Rishabh Shah was born on 17 December 1997 in Enfield, England, United Kingdom. 25 years old Rish was born to Upendra Shah and Sonal Shah, originally from Mumbai and Vadodara.

Rishabh has an elder sister but her name and other details are not available at the moment.

The multitalented actor was born into an Indian origin family. He grew up watching Bollywood movies.

Shah Rukh Khan was his inspiration for pursuing his career as an actor. He loved acting since his childhood.

Rish completed his basic education at Grange Park Primary School then he joined Haberdashers’ Boys School where he graduated his high school.

After high school, he joined Kings’s College London and completed his graduation in English Language and Linguistics.

To improve his acting skills he also joined Turbine Theatre.

Rish Shah- Professional Life, Careers

Rish Shah was fond of acting since his childhood. He participated in school dramas, and later he joined Turbine theater after high school.

The famous actor started auditioning in 2016 for movies. In this process, he had to move to New York.

He first got his project, the TV series “Prank Me” a Hazel Hayes project involving actors like Corey Fogelmanis, Lucy Robinson, and Rebecca Stone.

After his first project, he got a significant role after three years. In these three years, he worked on “Years and Years,” “Doctors,” and “Casualty.”

The actor got the role in “Emmerdale Farm” as Kirin Kotecha in 2020, which was a hit and won multiple awards, including a BAFTA award.

Later in 2021, he  worked with the famous Manisha Koirala in “India Sweets and Spices.”
