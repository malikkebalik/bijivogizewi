Xander Schauffele is a world-renowned golfer with numerous titles and accolades. The 4-time PGA winner has achieved a lot since turning professional in 2015. In fact, he is one of the top golf players in the world as of 2021. 

His personal life reflects his professional career as he got married to the love of his life. The pair had been together for a significant time before tying the knot. He regards his newlywed wife as his best friend, read all about their relationship and marriage below. 

Xander Schauffele And Maya Lowe Got Married In 2021

Schauffele and Maya Lowe decided to take their long-time relationship to the next level on Monday, July 5th, 2021. The couple had been dating for a number of years before they got engaged and eventually married. 

They started dating way back in 2014 and have been together ever since 2014. Each of them went to different universities but found time for each other and their relationship blossomed. 

A while later, they became inseparable and were seen attending events together. Soon, she came to many of his tours and accompanied him during his travels. 

The news of their relationship was quickly spread all over the golfing world. Xander seems to be infatuated with her in interviews and has even said that she is not only his wife but also his best friend. 

Xander and his wife live in Las Vegas, Nevada. They are enjoying a happy and fulfilled married life. They finally tied the knot after 7 years of dating. 

The couple saw each of their lives take off when they started their relationship which is why they are one of the sweetest couples in golf. The pair do not have any kids together as of July 2021.
